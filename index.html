Apr 15
Day 3 - 4 Sessions第3天 - 4场
US StockFX
US StockMM UserIn-person, 1h43m, Chinese

Profile: Chinese national in Japan (since 2005), multi-asset investor (JP+US stocks, crypto mining), left-side trader. Uses SBI for JP stocks, moomoo for US stocks. Rates moomoo US stock 100/100 vs SBI 30/100. Key findings: (1) PTS night trading is THE critical feature for JP stock migration. (2) Account unification (cash+margin buying power shared like SBI+bank) is essential — without it, users face unnecessary margin calls. (3) MMF auto-sweep for both JPY (~0.5%) and USD combined with PTS = "structural change" that would move ALL idle cash to moomoo. (4) Earnings AI needs smarter summarization (raw beat/miss can mislead — e.g. Saizeriya beat own forecast but missed analyst consensus). (5) Top 20-30 shareholder visibility per stock and margin trading data (信用買残/売残) are missing vs SBI. (6) Third-party portfolio apps (配当管理 etc.) only connect to SBI/Rakuten, creating hidden switching costs.画像：在日华人（2005年来日），多资产投资者（日美股+加密矿机），左侧交易风格。SBI做日股、moomoo做美股。moomoo美股打100分/SBI打30分。核心发现：(1) PTS夜间交易是日股迁移的决定性功能。(2) 账户统一（现金+信用买入力合并，如SBI+银行联动）是必须的——否则用户面临不必要的追保。(3) MMF自动申赎（日元约0.5%+美元）结合PTS="结构性变化"，会将所有闲置资金转入moomoo。(4) 决算AI需要更智能的摘要（raw beat/miss会误导——如萨莉亚超过自社预测但低于分析师共识）。(5) 个股前20-30大股东列表和信用取引数据（買残/売残）相比SBI缺失。(6) 第三方资产管理app（配当管理等）仅接入SBI/乐天，形成隐性切换成本。

"MMF + PTS combined is a structural change. I'd move ALL my idle cash to moomoo if both existed.""MMF + PTS 结合起来是结构性的变化。如果两个都有我会把所有闲置资金搬到moomoo。"

Switching Barriers Analysis切换壁垒分析

#1 Ecosystem Lock-in (Rakuten/SBI)#1 生态锁定（乐天/SBI）

Bank auto-sweep, credit card points, economic ecosystem integration. Mentioned in 15+ interviews.银行自动归集、信用卡积分、经济圈整合。15场以上访谈提及。

"The Rakuten bank sweep is so convenient that unless the rate is significantly better, I wouldn't switch.""楽天銀行の自動スイープが便利すぎて、よっぽど金利が良くない限り動かない。"
#2 Social Proof Deficit#2 缺乏社交证明

"Nobody around me uses moomoo." Japanese users heavily rely on word-of-mouth. Mentioned in 6+ interviews."周围没人用moomoo。"日本用户非常依赖口碑。6场以上访谈提及。

"I choose based on word-of-mouth and usability, not brand name. But very few people around me use moomoo.""口コミと使いやすさで選ぶ。でも周りにmoomoo使ってる人が非常に少ない。"
#3 Migration Friction#3 迁移摩擦

NISA transfer takes 3-4 months. Selling→withdrawing→depositing causes opportunity cost. Postal verification kills momentum.NISA转移需要3-4个月。卖出→出金→入金造成机会成本。邮寄验证消磨热情。

"By the time the postal verification arrives, my enthusiasm has already cooled off.""ハガキ届く頃にはもう熱が下がってる。"
#4 Brand Perception Gap#4 品牌认知偏差

"moomoo = US stocks only" or "too professional/speculative." This limits referrals and JP stock adoption."moomoo=只做美股"或"太专业/投机"。限制了转介和日股采用。

"The professional image is a double-edged sword. It scares off casual users.""プロっぽいイメージは諸刃の剣。カジュアルなユーザーが引いちゃう。"
#5 Third-party App Integration Gap#5 第三方App接入缺失

Portfolio management apps (配当管理, カビュウ etc.) only connect to SBI/Rakuten/Nomura. This creates hidden switching costs — users lose their consolidated portfolio view if they leave.资产管理类app（配当管理、カビュウ等）仅接入SBI/乐天/野村。形成隐性切换成本——用户离开后会失去统一的资产视图。

"Those dividend tracking apps only connect to SBI. If I switch, I lose my consolidated view.""那些配当管理app只接SBI。如果我换了就看不到统一视图了。"

Campaign Design Insights (from interviews)活动设计洞察（来自访谈）

What Works有效策略

  • +Guaranteed rewards > lottery: "Don't do 'up to 100K yen'. Just guarantee 10K. People are sick of lottery."确定性奖励 > 抽奖："不要'最高10万日元'。直接保证给1万。大家已经厌倦抽奖了。"
  • +Stock gifts on account opening: Multiple users cited "free stock" as appealing entry point开户送股：多位用户认为"免费股票"是有吸引力的入场点
  • +Low-barrier physical rewards: JFX's food campaigns (Wagyu beef, mango, curry) drive engagement without high trade volume requirements低门槛实物奖励：JFX的食品活动（和牛、芒果、咖喱）无需高交易量即可驱动参与
  • +Migration cashback (% based): "A few thousand yen won't move me. 1% cashback (cap 70-100K yen) would."转户返现（按比例）："几千日元我不会动。1%返现（上限7-10万）我会考虑。"

What Doesn't Work无效策略

  • -High-volume trade requirements: "The campaign requires millions in trades I'll never do"高交易量要求："活动要求我做不到的几百万交易量"
  • -Lottery-style "up to X" format: Creates distrust, users assume they'll get minimum抽奖式"最高X"格式：产生不信任感，用户默认自己只能拿到最低档
  • -Pure brand advertising without feature demo: Users need to see the product to be convinced纯品牌广告无功能演示：用户需要看到产品才能被说服
  • -IPO/speculative imagery: Creates "gambling" association that blocks referralsIPO/投机形象：造成"赌博"联想，阻碍用户转介
